Na'au Wala'au Designs

Naʻau Walaʻau Designs is a graphic design company that shares stories through products. When I first launched, I hand stamped ʻOhe Kāpala (bamboo stamping) t-shirts and kīhei. Because the demand started to grow, I started to sell screen printed t-shirts. From here, I grew into other products, such as hats, pareu, and eventually jewelry. Today, I focus mostly on Sterling Silver jewelry however we also produce traditional male clothing in old Hawaiʻi called Malo (loincloth). My goal at Naʻau Walaʻau Designs is to perpetuate Hawaiian culture and history through each of our pieces.
